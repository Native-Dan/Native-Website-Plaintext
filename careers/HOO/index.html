Head of Operations
Job Description
----------------
In an era where insurance innovation has often promised more than it's delivered, the industry finds itself in a state of inertia. Native has been created to engineer a new paradigm for Digital Assets insurance for the community, by the community.

As the first dedicated global broker in this space, we're building the bridge between the traditional insurance industry and on-chain finance. Our approach combines deep blockchain expertise with extensive insurance experience, using crypto-native technologies as our company’s foundation. We're not just adapting old models; we're creating a system that outlasts and outperforms into the future.

At Native, it’s different. We question established norms, not out of rebellion, but because we believe insurance can do more and be better. We're building a team of world-class individuals who bring fresh thinking to age-old problems. If you've ever felt out of step with traditional insurance practices, or if you've had ideas brushed aside because "that's not how we do things", we'd like to talk to you. Our duty is to reshape the status quo, not maintain it.

We’re looking for a Head of Operations to build the future of insurance from the ground up. With your industry-leading insurance operations knowledge, you'll be responsible for driving operational excellence across all departments and implementing innovative systems to support our capabilities. You hold yourself to a very high standard, deliver exceptional service and are relentless in identifying new opportunities that puts Native and our clients a step ahead of the rest. 

Requirements
-------------
- 10+ years in insurance, with 3+ years in operations
- Expertise in global binder management, Lloyd’s processes, tax and compliance
- Fluent with using and maintaining CRM software (e.g. Hubspot, Salesforce)
- A tenacious focus on driving operational excellence
- Strong drive to lead and collaborate on AI and automation initiatives
- Demonstrated ability to manage complex projects and deliver results
- An enduring passion for blockchain, AI and future emerging technologies

Responsibilities
-----------------
- Provide leadership for the Operations department and drive cross-departmental efficiency
- Collaborate with senior leadership to align operational strategies with overall business objectives and growth targets
- Develop and implement innovative operational systems in line with our blockchain and emerging technologies mandates
- Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and industry standards in all operational processes
- Develop and manage operational budgets, key performance indicators, and reporting mechanisms

Compensation
-------------
- Competitive salary with annual discretionary bonus
- EMI share scheme
- Comprehensive benefits package including pension
- 25 days annual leave plus statutory holidays
- Flexible working arrangements

How to Apply
-------------
Please send your resume and cover letter to careers@native.inc
